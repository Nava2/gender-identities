Let Your Users Represent Themselves Their Way

JSON Gender Identities is an open source and easy way to enable users of your app or website to express themselves the way that they chose to. Giving people the power of expression is something that we need to see more of, and through the power of the open source community you can be a force of change and make the Internet a better place for everyone.

Usage

Use the files identities.json and pronouns.json to populate your sign up form, user profile page, etc.
